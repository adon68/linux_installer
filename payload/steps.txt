Variables:

$PRODUCT
	Product name

$VERSION
	Full version number/name of the product

$APPLAUNCHER
	OS-specific name of the application launcher.
	Set the strings
	"_windows_app_launcher",
	"_linux_app_launcher" and
	"_mac_app_launcher"
	and the correct one will be inserted depending on the
	operating system the installer is run on.
	This should be the equivalent of "Start Menu" on Windows,
	"Application Menu" on Linux and "Dock" on Mac.
